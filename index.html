Incandescence is the annual cultural festival of the National Institute of Technology (NIT) Silchar. Known as one of the most vibrant and awaited events in the northeastern region, Incandescence is a celebration of art, culture, creativity, and youthful energy. It serves as a platform for students to showcase their talents through various cultural, literary, and artistic competitions, performances, and workshops.
